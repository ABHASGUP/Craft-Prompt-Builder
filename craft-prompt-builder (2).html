Tips for richer outputs

In Context, include real numbers — team size, timeframes, current metrics. Specificity anchors the AI to your actual problem.

A Role with named companies or niche specializations ("ex-Figma, design systems") signals deep domain expertise.

The Action should define the deliverable precisely — not just what to think about, but what to produce and at what depth.

Always set a Format — it forces the AI to structure its thinking and prevents vague walls of text.

Stack multiple Tones — combinations like "analytical + direct + strategic" produce the most actionable responses.
